Protocol Design

Protocol Design

Yield farming strategy (Work in Progress / Pending for final design document)

Determine whether the protocol meets the connect conditions

  • Is there an deposit or withdrawal fee 是否有存取费用
  • Whether the used stablecoin has a corresponding chainlink exchange rate. Chainlink exchange rate query address: https://docs.chain.link/docs/ethereum-addresses/ 使用到的稳定币是否有对应chainlink汇率。chainlink汇率查询地址：https://docs.chain.link/docs/ethereum-addresses/
  • The exchange platform and exchange path of mining coins and stablecoins are normal 矿币、稳定币的兑换平台和兑换路径正常
  • The third party has provided a query interface for asset evaluation or for net value 第三方有提供资产评估或者净值的查询接口
  • The third party supports money withdrawal, mines selling and reinvesting, and re-depositing in one transaction. 第三方支持在一笔交易内，顺序执行 取款，卖矿复投，再存款
  • Whether the protocol strategy has passed the audit, whether there is insurance, locked positions amount, and if they have the calculation method of APY on the official website 协议策略是否有通过审计，是否有保险，锁仓量，官网APY计算方式

After completing the above survey, you may enter the strategy development 做完以上调查后，可进入策略开发

Fund Redistribution - the calculation and processing

Variable definitions 变量定义：

  • durationDays：The cycle of rebalancing, which needs to ensure that in a cycle after rebalancing, the profit after rebalancing - the profit before rebalancing - the cost of rebalancing > 0 调仓的周期，需要保证在调仓后的一个周期内，调仓后的收益-调仓前的收益-调仓的成本>0
  • yearDays: days in a year 365 一年的天数，365
  • asset1：Strategy original assest 策略的原有资产
  • apr1：The strategy apr before the position adjustment (apy needs to be converted into apr), the current value of the apy of the position adjustment algorithm is the 7-day average of APY calculated outside the strategy chain 调仓前的策略apr（apy需要转换成apr），调仓算法的apy目前取值为策略链外计算APY的7天平均值
  • deltaAsset： capital change assumed value 假设策略调仓的资金变更值
  • poolAssets1: strategic target investment pool TVL, used in the algorithm as a parameter for the change of apr after the position adjustment 策略目标投资池子的TVL，算法里面用来作为调仓后apr变化的参数

For single strategy, the relationship between asset changes and income changes is 针对单个策略，资产变更和收益变更的关系为：

original income 原收益：gain1 = asset1 *apr1* durationDays / yearDays
Changed earnings 变更后的收益：gain2 = (asset1+deltaAsset-exchangeLoss)*apr2*durationDays/yearDays
Changed apr 变更后的apr：apr2=apr1*\frac{poolAssets1}{(poolAssets1+deltaAsset-exchangeLoss)}

After substituting apr2 in gain2 using the above equation 将gain2中的apr2使用上面的等式代入后：

gain2=\frac{apr1*durationDays/yearDays*(asset1+deltaAsset)*poolAssets1}{poolAssets1+deltaAsset-exchangeLoss}

Then the relationship between the changed income of a single strategy and the changed assets is 那么单个策略的变更后的收益和变更资产的关系为：

deltaGain = gain2-gain1 = deltaAsset*\frac{(poolAsset1-asset1)}{(poolAsset1+deltaAsset-exchangeLoss)}* apr1 *durationDays/yearDays

For single strategy, the cost of capital change is 单个策略，资金变更的成本为：

Withdrawal of funds operation gas fee 退出资金操作gas费

When deltaAsset<0, it means that the strategy is proposed

The operation fee is: withdrawFee = gasPrice * withdrawGas (withdrawGas is the gas consumed by the withdraw operation, which is estimated by pre-testing)
Additional Funding fee = additional funding operation gas fee + currency exchange slippage loss for invested funds
追加资金费用=追加资金操作gas费+投入资金的币种兑换滑点损失

When deltaAsset>0, it indicated a strategy investment

The operation fee is: lendFee = current gasPrice *lendGas (lendGas is the gas consumed by the lend operation, which is estimated by pre-testing)
The exchange loss is: exchangeLoss = deltaAsset* exchangeLossRate
(exchangeLossRate is currently set to the configured slippage upper limit of 0.3%. Since the actual exchange can be high or low, the same currency may not be exchanged, but the estimated loss is calculated based on the configured slippage value)

在deltaAsset>0时，表示策略投入

操作费用为：lendFee = 当前gasPrice *lendGas（lendGas为lend操作消耗的gas，通过预先测试预估得到的）
兑换损失为：exchangeLoss = deltaAsset* exchangeLossRate
（exchangeLossRate当前设置为配置好的滑点上限0.3%，由于实际兑换可高可低，同币种也可能不兑换，但是预估计算时，是以配置的滑点值来算损失）

Funding remains unchanged 资金不变

When deltaAsset is 0, it indicated no action and thus no operation cost is required. 在deltaAsset为0时，表示不动，无操作费用

Harvest Funding Fees

If the strategy retains funds after the position adjustment, it is necessary for the fund to perform Harvest selling and reinvesting during the cycle, and the required Harvest operation costs are:

harvestFee = HarvestGas *durationDays (currently, harvest is performed every day in the assumed period)

当策略在调仓后还存留资金，则周期内还需要执行Harvest卖矿复投，需要Harvest操作费用为：

harvestFee = HarvestGas* durationDays（当前是假设周期内每天执行harvest）

The cost of changing the funds in the buffer pool is 缓冲池资金变更的成本为：

For buffer pool funds, it is considered that:
When fetching from the buffer pool:
withdrawFee = 0
When putting into the buffer pool:
lendFee = 0
exchangeLoss =0
Buffer pool without Harvest
harvestFee=0

Q&A 问题求解

When the requirement is the maximum sum of the deltaGain of all strategies:

Total Change Gain = Sum(deltaGain -operateFee - exchangeLoss-harvestFee) =sum(deltaAsset*\frac{(poolAsset-asset)}{(poolAsset+deltaAsset-exchangeLoss)}* apr * durationDays/yearDays - operateFee - exchangeLoss - harvestFee)
In this formula, the only variable is the deltaAsset of each strategy

那么我们要求的就是，所有策略的deltaGain的总和最大值：

总变更收益 = Sum(deltaGain -operateFee - exchangeLoss-harvestFee) =sum(deltaAsset*\frac{(poolAsset-asset)}{(poolAsset+deltaAsset-exchangeLoss)}* apr * durationDays/yearDays - operateFee - exchangeLoss - harvestFee)
该公式中，唯一的变量为每个策略的deltaAsset

Restrictions 约束条件：

  The funds of the same protocol strategy (multiple constraints) do not exceed 30% of the total funds. For example, 1 and 2 strategies are the same as the protocol:
  totalAsset * 0.3 - Asset1 - deltaAsset1 -Asset2-deltaAsset2 >=0
  The sum of all asset changes in and out is 0: sum(deltaAsset) = 0

Boundary conditions 边界条件：

Other asset constraints related to the size boundary calculation conditions of deltaAsset, are as follow:

  • Strategic assets cannot exceed 20% of total assets
  • The strategic funds cannot exceed 50% of the target pool assets
  • The additional investment assets of the strategy cannot exceed the investable quota

Therefore, the minimum value of deltaAsset is to exit all existing assets of the strategy, and the maximum value is the maximum investable asset after the above conditions limit.

其他资产限制条件，作为deltaAsset的大小边界计算条件，有

  • 策略资产不能超过总资产20%
  • 策略资金不能超过目标池子资产的50%
  • 策略追加投入资产不能超过可投资配额

所以，deltaAsset最小为退出所有策略现有资产，最大值为上面的条件限制后的最大可投资资产
